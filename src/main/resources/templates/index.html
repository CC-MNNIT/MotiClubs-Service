Welcome to MotiClubs

Tired of too many groups on WhatsApp ? MotiClubs is your gateway to streamlined club and community communication at MNNIT Allahabad. We are here to empower your club with a platform designed for all students, enhancing inclusivity and efficiency.

Efficient Communication

MotiClubs lets you create channels and connect with members based on specific criteria such as year, branch, or course. Make announcements, share posts with images, and effortlessly contact anyone in the college community by clicking on their profile.

Inclusivity Matters

We understand that inclusivity is key. MotiClubs breaks down barriers and welcomes all students. No more dependency on exclusive platforms. Keep everyone in the loop, regardless of their status or affiliation.

Channel Control

Take control of your channels with MotiClubs. Create both private and public channels, allowing respective users to engage and collaborate seamlessly while ensuring data privacy.

Club Communication

Simplify club management, collaborate seamlessly, and keep everyone in the loop, regardless of their affiliation. MotiClubs ensures that your club's announcements, events, and discussions are organized and easily accessible.
